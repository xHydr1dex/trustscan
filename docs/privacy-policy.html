TrustScan Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Short version: TrustScan does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. Review text is sent to our AI backend solely to generate a trust score and is never retained.

What TrustScan does

TrustScan is a Chrome extension that analyses customer reviews on e-commerce websites (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Meesho) and assigns each review a trust score using rule-based checks, machine learning, and an LLM judge.

Data we collect

TrustScan does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account credentials.
  • We do not track your browsing history or the pages you visit.
  • We do not store or log the review text you submit for analysis.
  • We do not use cookies or any cross-site tracking mechanisms.

Data sent to our servers

When you click Scan Reviews, the extension sends the following data to our analysis API (xhydr1dex-trustscan-api.hf.space):

  • The text of the visible reviews on the current product page
  • The star rating, verified purchase status, and whether media is attached — as extracted from the page
  • An anonymised reviewer ID derived from the public profile URL (not your identity)

This data is processed in real time to produce a trust score and is not stored, logged, or retained by our servers after the response is sent.

Local storage

TrustScan uses chrome.storage.local to maintain a local reviewer knapsack on your device. This stores anonymised reviewer–product pairs (e.g. reviewer IDs and product ASINs you have scanned) to enable ring detection across sessions. This data:

  • Never leaves your browser unless you explicitly trigger a scan
  • Is stored only on your device
  • Can be cleared at any time by removing the extension

Third-party services

Our analysis API uses Groq (LLaMA 3.3 70B) for LLM-based review analysis. Review text sent to our API may be forwarded to Groq's API for inference. Groq's privacy policy is available at groq.com/privacy-policy. Groq does not use API inputs to train models.

Permissions we request

  • storage — to save the local reviewer knapsack for ring detection
  • activeTab — to read review content from the tab you are currently viewing
  • host permissions (amazon.in, amazon.com, flipkart.com, myntra.com, meesho.com) — to inject the scan button and read reviews on supported platforms

We request only the minimum permissions necessary. We do not request access to your browsing history, cookies, or any other sensitive data.

Children's privacy

TrustScan is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and release a new version of the extension. Continued use of TrustScan after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
